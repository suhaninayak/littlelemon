About Me

Based in Mumbai, India, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean-Indian fusion restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chef, Leonardo Nair, raised in an Italian-Indian household, inherited his love for food from his father, an Italian chef, and his mother, an Indian home cook. Their diverse culinary traditions inspired him to create a unique menu blending the bold flavors of both regions.

The restaurant has a warm and inviting ambiance with moderate prices, making it a popular spot for both locals and travelers seeking an authentic yet innovative dining experience.

Little Lemon is Chef Leonardo's passion project, born from his journey of embracing both his Italian heritage and his deep connection to Indian cuisine. After training in Italy, he moved to India to bring this fusion concept to life.

Drawing from his family recipes and years of experience, he curates a seasonal menu that beautifully marries Mediterranean staples with Indian spices and techniques.

To introduce this unique blend to a wider audience, he spearheaded a marketing campaign that celebrates the restaurant's fusion concept, making Little Lemon a beloved culinary destination.

Join us at Little Lemon for a delightful culinary journey that transcends borders and tantalizes your taste buds.
